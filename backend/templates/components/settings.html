Enable Web Service Screenshots

When enabled, reconYa will automatically capture screenshots of web services running on discovered devices. Screenshots help identify web applications and their interfaces.

Note: Settings are automatically saved when changed. Disabling screenshots will hide them from device details and prevent new screenshots from being captured during scans.
